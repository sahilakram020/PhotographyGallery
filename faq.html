Frequently Asked Questions

What type of photography do you specialize in?

I specialize in outdoor, portrait, wildlife, and abstract photography, capturing beautiful moments and telling stories through my lens.

How do I book a session with you?

You can book a session by contacting me directly through the Contact Page or emailing me at sahilakram2007@gmail.com. I’ll be happy to discuss your needs and schedule a shoot.

Do you provide digital images or printed photos?

Not now, soon this feature will be launched.Stay tuned.

How far in advance should I book a session?

It’s recommended to book at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons. However, I will always try to accommodate last-minute requests if my schedule allows.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes, you can reschedule your session with a minimum of 48 hours' notice. In case of emergencies, please contact me as soon as possible.
